Welcome to MySleep

We provide continuous and non-intrusive monitoring of your sleep for detecting respiratory events and sleep disorders

Continuous Monitoring

Allows daily monitoring of your night's sleep quality.

Non-intrusive Process

Non-intrusive sensing for greater comfort at night.

Mobile Health Solution

A user-friendly application, easy to monitoring and result analysis.

Cloud Computing

Using the power of Cloud Computing to process and analyse the data.

Signal Processing

The data collected are processed using advanced signal processing techniques.

Machine Learning

A Machine learning model is built to automatically predict sleep disorders.

How it Works

  • 01

    Monitoring your sleep

    Instructions are given in the application to help you to perform the data recording.

  • 02

    Cloud Computing

    The signals captured are transferred and processed by noval algorithms at cloud.

  • 03

    Sleep metrics for each monitored night

    Sleep quality metrics and sleep disorders indicatives are presented in the application.

  • 04

    Sleep centers have access to sleep metrics

    Sleep centers can have access to dashboards presenting the sleep data of their patients through MySleep portal.

About

MySleep

We aim to improve the sleep quality and disorders monitoring, helping to track the evolution and treatment follow up. We believe in the power of mobile devices to provide an auxiliary tool for preliminary diagnosis and treatment monitoring.

Performing research on the state-of-the-art of detection of respiratory events, we are able to process and analyze the respiratory events not intrusively.
